INTRODUCTION

Another important aspect of our service line is the procurement and supply of equipment, machinery, instruments, tools, spares, materials, chemicals, other consumable, etc. to the Energy and Power, Oil and Gas, Manufacturing, Construction support service and allied sectors of the Nigerian economy. DILA has attained a high level of success in this direction, having continuously made its operations to be compatible with the extensive volume and unimaginable varieties of procurable supplies, regularly and sometimes, spontaneously ordered by many of our clients. Be it locally or internationally, we source our products from reputable manufacturers or their appointed representatives. This has sustained our growing tradition of servicing every purchase order with very high quality and standard products, to the utmost satisfaction of our highly valued clients. As part of our procurement and supply development effort, we have taken advantage of the current global information order - the Internet. Interestingly, it has contributed drastically to the reduction of our service period vis-à-vis our clients’ foreign purchase orders. In addition, this exploit together with our good relationships with local and overseas product manufacturers and their agents have to some extent, made our product prices to be moderate and competitive. By positioning our good selves as an engineering, logistics, manpower support and procurement company, we have always provided our clients with a totally integrated, systematic and contemporary services at the best affordable costs.

The scope of our procurement and supply operations have are outlined as follows:

  • Local & foreign procurement
  • Bulk supplies
  • Regular supplies
  • Supplies for turnkey projects
  • Supplies for plant turn around maintenance
  • Stop-gap supplies for Rush order servicing
  • Call off [long term] procurement contracts
  • Inventory management & advisory services
  • Product market development services
  • Procurement facilitation & allied services
  • Commissioned agents for technical products
